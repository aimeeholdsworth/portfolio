Design Work

Throughout my time at university I worked on a number of audio visual, videography projects.

Product Trailer and Ident


In my third year I worked within a group to bring to life a concept for a short trailer for a futuristic company. My group and I opted to create a typically cheesy advert for a virtual wardrobe product that let you change looks on the go. I worked on all elements of production, including pre and post production. Using Premiere Pro we put together footage we had filmed and I created visual overlays, enhanced footage and edited stop motion animation to help better tell the story. Using After Effects I created an Ident for the project using bold and graphic shapes to catch the viewers attention and portray the futuristic element of the project.

Kickstarter Video


In my final year I worked with a fellow student to create a kickstarter video for a business idea as part of our coursework. Using a mix of our own and stock footage we developed a short trailer to portray the product the company was selling and encourage viewers to buy. This built upon Premiere Pro skills I had developed of enchancing video footage, editing out backgrounds and creating bold visuals using animation.
